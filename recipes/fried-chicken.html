Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken, also known as Southern fried chicken, is a dish consisting of chicken pieces that have been coated with seasoned flour or batter and pan-fried, deep fried, pressure fried, or air fried. The breading adds a crisp coating or crust to the exterior of the chicken while retaining juices in the meat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken drumsticks
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Combine the flour, paprika, salt, and pepper in a zip-top bag. Seal the bag and shake until well-combined.
  2. Dredge the chicken in the buttermilk. Then, coat the chicken with the breading
  3. Fry the chicken at high heat. Then, drain the chicken by using paper towels
