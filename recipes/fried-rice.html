Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fri ed in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with othe r ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. I t is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • White onion
  • Vegetables
  • Eggs
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Green Onions

Instructions

  1. Preheat skillet at medium-high heat
  2. Put veggies and beaten eggs into skillet
  3. Add rice along with soy sauce
  4. Garnish with green onions
  5. Plate and serve
